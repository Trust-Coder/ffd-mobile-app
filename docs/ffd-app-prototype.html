Monsoon Flood Advisory — Chenab & Jhelum catchments

A monsoon system over the upper catchments of the Chenab and Jhelum is expected to bring moderate to heavy rainfall over the next 36 hours. Flows are forecast to rise into the medium flood range at Marala and Khanki, with a chance of brief high flood peaks.
